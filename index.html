ABOUT VITMAS

VITMAS (VIT Mathematical Association) is one of the most distinguished & diligent technical chapter in VIT. We are not just an organization, we are an enthusiastic family, united by one cause, working towards making technology more accessible, comprehensible & implementable. The students of VITMAS have been working on projects related to web development, graphic designing, machine learning, IOT, app development and a lot more, and has been organising various outreach programs to educate the underprivileged as much as our humble span of knowledge permits. Apart from this, VITMAS also boasts of excellent connections in real time industries and also many faculty coordinators – one of the few chapters in VIT to possess so. The students of VITMAS happen to be great learners and skilled technicians – who’ve conquered multiple hacks in and around VIT, won commendable research awards and also have a lot of experience and projects at hand to share to budding engineers.
